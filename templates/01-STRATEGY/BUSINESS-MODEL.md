## 8. Claude Code Usage Notes

Claude Code should help with:

- clarifying the business model
- identifying growth opportunities
- simplifying operations
- improving customer communication
- creating strategic action plans
- identifying what to stop, start, or systemise

Claude Code should avoid:

- giving generic business advice
- suggesting complex automation too early
- making assumptions without flagging them
- overcomplicating simple business problems

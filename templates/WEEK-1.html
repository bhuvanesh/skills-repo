Week 1 · Foundation

Start here.

These files give Claude context about your business so its answers fit your situation instead of being generic. Spend a few hours filling them in this week. You'll get the time back many times over.

What to expect

About 3–5 hours total, split across two or three sittings. One sitting tends to get sloppy by the end.

You don't need to fill every field. If you don't know an answer, leave it blank or write unknown and come back later.

How to fill them

Guided by Clauderecommended

Claude reads the file, asks you one section at a time, and writes your answers in. For open-ended questions, it offers a few examples to react to — and asks if those are helping. If they're not, it switches to plain questions.

$ /onboarding-help BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md

On your own

Open the file in your editor. Type after each Label: line. Skip what you don't know.

When you're done

You don't need every field filled. You're done when these three things are true:

  • Claude can describe your business in one sentence without making things up.
  • The three 90-day priorities you wrote down feel like the right ones.
  • A new team member could read BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md and understand the business in ten minutes.

A few rules

  • One file at a time. Don't jump around.
  • Short answers are usually enough.
  • Use your customer's own words, not your version of them.
  • unknown is a valid answer.
  • Re-read each file once before you close it.
